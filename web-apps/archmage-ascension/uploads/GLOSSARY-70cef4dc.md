**GLOSSARY**

**A**

**Ambient magic** *(Story)* The raw, natural magical energy flowing
through the universe. Exists unseen in the aether until drawn into the
Source or crystallized in the Array.

**The Array** *(Mechanical)* The 5 face-up components maintained by the
Academy. When a component is taken from the Array, ambient magic from
the Source immediately crystallizes to replace it. During
Transfiguration, wizards claim Array components by using hand components
as catalysts. Collapses into the Released Reserve when the Drought
begins.

**The Arcane Reserve** *(Mechanical)* The discard pile. A containment
matrix that captures drained magical energy from Transfiguration
catalysts. All players bind to it during the Opening. Merges with the
Array to form the Released Reserve when the Drought begins.

**The Ascension** *(Mechanical + Story)* The final phase triggered when
the Released Reserve empties. The magic automatically evaluates all
Spellbooks and elevates the most powerful wizard to Archmage. This is a
magical inevitability, not a judged competition.

**Attuned** *(Story)* When a wizard controls a component (in hand), they
understand its element and resonance frequency. The component is attuned
to that wizard.

**The aether** *(Story)* The universal field through which ambient magic
flows. Used as poetic/flavor synonym for \"the Source.\"

**C**

**Capacity** *(Mechanical)* How many spells you can cast per turn and
how many learning actions you can perform. Represented by counters.
Starts at 1, increases by learning Enchantments, decreases by breaking
Enchantments apart.

**Cast** *(Mechanical)* To activate a spell from your Spellbook during
the Casting Phase by placing a counter on it. Effects resolve
immediately. Each spell can be cast once per turn maximum.

**Casting Phase** *(Mechanical)* The second phase of each turn (during
normal play only). Choose which spells from your Spellbook to activate.
Place counters on them one at a time and resolve effects immediately.
Does not exist during the Drought.

**Catalysts** *(Story)* Components used in Transfiguration to claim
Array components. They\'re drained but not destroyed---their energy
flows to the Arcane Reserve.

**Collection Phase** *(Mechanical + Story)* The first phase of each
turn. Draw 1 component from the Source OR take 1 component from the
Array (immediately replace from Source). During the Drought, draw
exactly 1 from the Released Reserve (no choice).

**Component** *(Mechanical)* A card representing a fragment of magical
energy. Has an element (Fire, Water, Earth, Air) and a value/resonance
frequency (1-20).

**Conjuration** *(Mechanical)* A spell type using 3+ components of the
same element (any values). When cast, draws additional components from
the Source: +1 for 3-5 card spell, +2 for 6+ card spell. Cannot be cast
during the Drought.

**Counter** *(Mechanical)* Physical token tracking your magical
capacity. Used during Casting Phase (determines how many spells you can
cast) and Learning Phase (determines how many learning actions you can
perform). Returned to your pool via the Recall step at the start of the
Learning Phase — they represent capacity, not a spent resource.

**Crystallization** *(Story)* The process by which ambient magic becomes
visible and tangible in the Array. Maintained by the Academy\'s
enchantments.

**D**

**Drained** *(Story)* When components are used as catalysts in
Transfiguration, they\'re depleted of energy but not destroyed. Drained
components flow to the Arcane Reserve where their energy can renew.

**Draw** *(Mechanical)* Take the top card from the Source and add it to
your hand.

**The Drought** *(Mechanical + Story)* The phase triggered when the
Source empties. The Array and Arcane Reserve merge into the Released
Reserve. The Casting Phase no longer exists---external spellcasting
becomes impossible. Only Collection and Learning phases remain.

**E**

**Element** *(Mechanical)* One of the four fundamental forces: Fire,
Water, Earth, Air. Represented by card suit symbols (♠♥♦♣).

**Empower** *(Mechanical)* A learning action. Add one or more components
from your hand to an existing spell in your Spellbook. Costs 1 counter
(or free with unlimited capacity).

**Enchantment** *(Mechanical + Story)* A spell type using 3-4 components
of matching value (any elements). Always active---never needs to be
cast. Permanently increases capacity: 3-component grants +1 counter,
4-component grants unlimited capacity. Represents internal metamorphosis
woven into the wizard\'s being.

**External magic** *(Story)* Magic that requires ambient flow and stable
infrastructure (Conjurations, Transfigurations, Perfect Transmutations).
Cannot function during the Drought.

**H**

**Hand** *(Mechanical)* Your cards. Components you\'ve attuned and
control, ready to be used to learn spells or as catalysts.

**I**

**Infrastructure** *(Story)* The magical systems maintained by the
Academy (the Source\'s flow, the Array\'s crystallization). Collapses
when the Source empties, triggering the Drought.

**Internal magic** *(Story)* Magic woven into the wizard\'s own essence
(Enchantments). Persists even during the Drought because it\'s
independent of external infrastructure.

**L**

**Learn** *(Mechanical)* A learning action. Place a new valid spell from
your hand into your Spellbook. Costs 1 counter (or free with unlimited
capacity). When learning a 3-component Enchantment, immediately gain +1
counter. When learning a 4-component Enchantment, immediately gain
unlimited capacity.

**Learning Phase** *(Mechanical)* The third phase of each turn. After
casting (or during Drought, after collection), you perform the Recall
step — pick up all counters from cast spells and return them to your
pool — then spend them on learning actions. Use counters to Learn,
Empower, Reshape, or Unlearn spells. Works normally even during the
Drought.

**O**

**The Opening** *(Mechanical + Story)* Before the first turn, each
player simultaneously chooses 1 component from hand and discards it to
the Arcane Reserve. This binds all players to the Reserve, allowing it
to capture drained energy throughout the game.

**P**

**Pattern / Configuration** *(Story)* The arrangement of components in
the Spellbook. Each pattern proves understanding of a magical principle.
Can be activated when infrastructure exists (casting) or stands as proof
of knowledge when it doesn\'t (during Drought).

**Perfect Transmutation** *(Mechanical + Story)* A spell type using 3+
components in sequential values, all from the same element. When cast,
provides BOTH Conjuration effect (draw from Source) AND Transfiguration
effect (drain catalysts, claim from Array). Both effects are mandatory.
Represents absolute mastery within one element. Cannot be cast during
the Drought.

**R**

**Recall** *(Mechanical)* The transition step between Casting Phase and
Learning Phase. Pick up all counters from your cast spells and return
them to your pool. This physical act signals the phase change — the same
counters now determine your learning actions. Players with unlimited
capacity skip the Recall (their Unlimited Capacity marker already
signals their state).

**The Released Reserve** *(Mechanical)* The final draw pile, formed when
the Drought begins by shuffling the Array and Arcane Reserve together.
Limited, unstable magical energy for final preparations. When depleted,
triggers the Ascension.

**Reshape** *(Mechanical)* A learning action. Reorganize components
across multiple spells in your Spellbook. Break down any/all spells and
rebuild using the same components. All components must remain in valid
spell arrangements. Costs 1 counter (or free with unlimited capacity).
Counts as one action regardless of how many spells affected. Warning:
breaking apart Enchantments reduces capacity.

**Resonance** *(Story + Mechanical)* The power measurement used during
the Ascension. Each spell pattern generates resonance strength based on
its size and type.

**Resonance frequency** *(Mechanical + Story)* The numbered value (1-20)
on each component. Represents the specific vibrational pattern within an
element. Used interchangeably with \"value.\"

**S**

**The Source** *(Mechanical)* The face-down deck containing raw ambient
magic. During Collection Phase, draw from the Source or take from the
Array (which immediately replaces from the Source). When the Source
empties, the Drought begins.

**Spellbook** *(Mechanical + Story)* Your tableau---the area in front of
you where you arrange components into learned spells. This arrangement
proves your mastery and will be evaluated during the Ascension.

**T**

**Take** *(Mechanical)* Claim a component from the Array and add it to
your hand. The Academy immediately replaces it by drawing from the
Source (making ambient magic crystallize).

**Transfiguration** *(Mechanical + Story)* A spell type using 3+
components in sequential values (any elements). When cast, must exchange
with the Array: drain hand components as catalysts to claim Array
components. 3-component spell: discard 2 from hand, take 1 from Array.
4+ component spell: discard 1 from hand, take 1 from Array. Drained
components flow to Arcane Reserve. Cannot be cast during the Drought.

**U**

**Unlimited capacity** *(Mechanical)* State granted by learning a
4-component Enchantment. Cast all spells per turn, perform unlimited
learning actions per turn. Place the Unlimited Capacity marker in front
of you and set all counters aside. Lost if that 4-component Enchantment
is broken apart — return the marker and retrieve your counters.
