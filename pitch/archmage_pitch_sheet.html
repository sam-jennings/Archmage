ARCHMAGE ASCENSION

A Strategic Card Game of Magical Mastery

"The Archmage is gone. One must ascend."

Magic is fading. Without an Archmage to anchor the arcane currents, the world's power dwindles. The Grand Academy has summoned its greatest wizards to prove their mastery. Gather mystical components, learn powerful spells, and build your Spellbook. When the Vault runs dry and chaos erupts, the wizard with the greatest magical prowess will ascend.
Game Overview
WHO: You are a powerful wizard competing against rival mages at the Grand Academy.
WHAT: Collect elemental components (cards) and arrange them into spell patterns in your Spellbook—sets of matching elements, runs of sequential values, or matching values.
HOW: Cast spells to draw more components and transform unwanted cards. When the deck empties, your Spellbook is evaluated. The wizard with the highest mastery becomes the Archmage!
Two ways to win: Quick scoring OR the strategic Ascension Trial for deeper competition
Four Spell Types
🔮 Conjuration
Same element (3+). Draw bonus components from the Vault.
🔄 Transfiguration
Sequential values (3+). Exchange components with the Array.
⚡ Enchantment
Matching values (3-4). Permanently boost your capacity.
✨ Perfect Transmutation
Sequential + same element. Grants BOTH benefits!
